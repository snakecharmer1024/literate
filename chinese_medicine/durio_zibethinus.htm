durian 榴槤

 

 by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

Durio zibethinus Murray

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

lau4 lin4

Common Name:

英文名﹕

durian

Distribution:

產地﹕

Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam and Northern Australia

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

甜﹐刺激性﹐燥﹐有小毒。

sweet, pungent, hot and mildly toxic

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

kidney

腎

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Besides eaten as a snack, it is also being used as
  1. Tonic for deficiency, for cold type of body.
  2. Rid of cold phlegm.
  3. Expels intestine worms
  4. Decoction of the leaves and roots is used as antipyretic
  5. The leaf juice is applied on the head of a fever patient.
  6. The leaves are employed in medicinal baths for people with jaundice.
  7. Decoctions of the leaves and fruits are applied to swellings and skin diseases.
  8. The ash of the burned rind is taken after childbirth.
  9. The odor of the flesh is believed to be linked to indole compounds which are bacteriostatic.
  10. Improve sexual function for those who are shenyangxu (kidney yang deficient)腎陽虛

 

. See "Cautions" item below.

主治與功用﹕

  1. 體質虛弱者﹐可作為進補用﹐
  2. 可增加體溫﹐驅寒痰﹐
  3. 驅腸內寄生蟲﹐
  4. 葉或根煎湯服作退熱劑用
  5. 葉搗汁﹐外敷於額作退熱劑用
  6. 葉用水煎﹐作浴水﹐用於黃疸病人
  7. 葉與果作煎劑﹐用於皮膚病
  8. 果皮燒灰﹐產婦服用作為調理身體之用
  9. 榴槤之氣味一般相信與抑制細菌的引朵 (糞臭素 ) 有關

 看以下禁忌條。

化學成份﹕

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

  1. Not to take in cases of yinxu (yin deficient) , diabetic,  hypertension, or fever.
  2. Do not take with alcohol.

 

  1. 陰虛  有熱﹐糖尿病﹐血壓高﹐發熱﹐癌症 患者不宜吃。

  2. 不可在吃榴槤時同時喝酒。
