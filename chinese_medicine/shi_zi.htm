Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

 Persimmons contain tannin acid. If eaten with high protein food like meat or fish, the protein can be hardened and can form tannin albumate and is difficult to digest. In some people it can create constipation.

Because persimmons are cold in characteristics, people with cold type of body (yang deficient) should avoid eating them.

Over consumption of persimmons can cause stercolith (fecal stones) in the intestines [2]. Stercolith can cause constipation or even can cause gastro intestine perforation.[1]

Note: there is little research on the polyphenols of persimmon in the West because it is not a popular food item among the Westerners.

柿子含鞣酸如與肉類一起食用，會產生一種鞣酸蛋白，使得肉類裡的蛋白質凝固而不易消化。甚至會產生便秘。柿子性寒性，體質偏寒(陽 虛)者忌吃。

吃柿子太多會引起糞石【2】。糞石能導致便秘，甚至能貫穿腸胃。【1】

注: 在西方，對柿子之多酚類的研究難見到，因為柿子在西方不是主要的食物。

[1] J Coll Physicians Surg Pak. 2012 Jul;22(7):455-7. doi: 07.2012/JCPSP.455457.

[2]  Source: Chen Xuanwei, director of Pediatrics ofTaizhong city (Taiwan) Hospital,資料來源：台中市陳鉉煒小兒科院長（作者亦為新生兒科專科醫師)

 

Note:

The white powder on the surface of the dried persimmons sold are usually titanium oxide added to the the fruit during processing. This added white powder is loose and can fall off easily.  The naturally occurs white powder during the drying process does not fall off easily.

 

柿餅的白霜是通常經過人工“打粉（加了鈦白，主要成分是二氧化鈦）。人工“打粉的柿餅柿霜層鬆散、 容易脫落、粘手，上霜的情況很普遍，而自然出霜的柿餅的白霜則不易脫落，不同個體上霜的程度各有差別。
