tuo hua jian 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

　

Uses: difficulty in child birth delivery or dead fetus.

　

Prescription:

dang gui 26g to 38 g, rou gui 4g to 12 g,

chuan xiong 8 g, niu xi 8 g, che qian zi 6 g,

hong hua 4 g.

　

Make into decoction and use while warm.

　

Source of formula: Classic, Jing Yue Quan Shu

脫花煎  (出處﹕景岳全書)

凡臨盆將產者,宜先服此藥催生最佳,并治產難經日至死胎不下。

當歸,七錢至一兩﹐肉桂一錢至三錢﹐

川芎, 牛膝各二錢﹐

車前子錢半﹐紅花一錢。

水煎溫服。

註﹕一錢 = 3.75  克 (舊制)
