san yin jiao point SP6 三陰交 (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯                按此看中文   

san yin jiao point

Location: In the medial malleolus tip (inner bulge of the ankle) going straight up 3 cun , at the posterior margin of the tibia.

Application: Genitourinary disorders, uterine bleeding, vaginal discharge, orchitis, acute and chronic nephritis, Dysuria, gonorrhea, intestinal colic, impotence, spermatorrhea, enuresis, acute and chronic enteritis, thromboangiitis obliterans, hypertension, insomnia, eczema, urticaria, skin irritation, diabetes, neurosis, toxic dyspepsia, prolonged labor.

Method: straight insertion 1 to 1.5 cun.

定位：在內踝尖直上三寸，脛骨後緣。

主治：泌尿生殖系疾患，子宮出血，帶下，睪丸炎，急慢性腎炎，癃閉，淋病，腸疝痛，陽萎，遺精，遺尿，急慢性腸炎，血栓閉塞性脈管炎，高血壓，失眠，濕疹，蕁麻疹，皮膚騷痒，糖尿病、神經官能症，中毒性消化不良，滯產。

刺法：直刺 1∼1.5寸
