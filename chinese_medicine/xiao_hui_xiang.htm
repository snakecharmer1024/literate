xiao hui xiang 小茴香  (in English and Chinese 中英文)
by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Fructus Foeniculi Vulgaris

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

Foeniculum vulgare Mill.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

shouikyo

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

sohoehyang

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

siu2 wui4 heong1

Other Names:

別名﹕

xiao hui 小茴﹐huai xiang 蘹香 ﹐hui xiang 茴香﹐

hui xiang zi 茴香子。 Adas (Malay, Indonesian)

Common Name:

英文名﹕

fennel

Distribution:

產地﹕

throughout China and south east Asia

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

warm, acrid

性溫,味辛。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

  1. anti fungal effect [2]. Fennel oil samples have shown high activity against Candida albicans.[5]

  2. antioxidant [1][7]

  3. releases stored fat for consumption of the body [3]

  4. potential in preventing bone loss in postmenopausal osteoporosis by reducing both osteoclast differentiation and function.[4]

  5. prevent rat hepatic fibrosis by reducing amount of collagen fiber and improve hepatic functions.[6]

  6. emmenagogue effects (promote menses blood flow)

  7. increases kidney activity and decreases urea rate [9]

  8. increases progesterone [10]

  9. gel of 3% fenel decreased hair thickness in idiopathic mild to moderate hirsutism [10]

  10. Aqueous extract of fennel seed showed the beneficial effect (especially at dose of 150 mg/kg b.w.) on renal function in in rats with poly cystic ovarian syndrome. [11]

  11. possesses oestrogenic activity [12]

 

  1. 抗真菌 作用 [2]. 小茴香之精油對白色念珠球菌有很強的對抗作用 [5].

  2. 抗氧化 作用 [1]

  3. 釋放儲 存的脂肪以供身體消耗 [3]

  4. 可能有 預防在絕經後, 減少破骨細胞之分化作用而防止 骨質疏 鬆症之潛能 [4]

  5. 減少大 鼠肝膠原纖維的量而防止肝纖維化，和改善肝功能。[6]

  6. 通經作用（促進月經血流量）

  7. 增進腎臟機能, 降低尿素 [9]

  8. 增加黃體素[10]

  9. 3％ 的小茴香凝膠對特發性輕度至中度多毛症能降低毛體的粗度[10]

  10. 小茴香的水提物對患有多囊性卵巢綜合征的大鼠之對腎功能有益作用
    （特別是在150毫克/ kg體重之劑量）。 [11]

  11. 有女性性激素的作用[12]

Channels (meridians) entered:

歸經﹕

liver, kidney, spleen, stomach

肝﹐腎﹐脾﹐胃。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

  1. Cold type of hernia,

  2. Indigestion, gastric gas and colic , especially for babies.

  3. Swollen testes.

  4. Cholera and vomiting

  5. Beriberi

  6. For respiratory congestion and cough

  7. Frequent urination

  8. For stimulating the flow of breast milk.

  9. External use in abdominal pain: stir fry in pan until hot and wrap in cloth and apply to abdomen area for pain and cold sensation.

  10. The xiao hui xiang gel of 3% can reduce the thickness of facial hair [10]

 

  1. 寒 疝腹痛

  2. 胸腹脹滿, 消化不良, 鼓腸氣脹, 腸絞痛, 常用於嬰兒。

  3. 睪丸腫痛

  4. 霍亂嘔吐

  5. 胃炎食少

  6. 腳氣

  7. 小便數

  8. 催乳

  9. 外敷﹕ 腹部冷痛﹐ 炒熱銷茴﹐布包﹐熱敷於腹部。

  10. 小茴香3％凝膠的效果與安慰劑相比，顯明 能減少的面部毛髮粗度(由顯微鏡探查)。[10]

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

  • Use caution in cases of yinxu (yin deficient) and lack of liquid.

  • Not recommended for pregnant women with weak constitution or use with care when using it. because the herb has estrogenic activity.

  • may not suitable to use with Fluoroquinolone drugs

  • The estrogenic activity of this herb brings some side effects such as decrease in protein concentration and acid and alkaline phosphatase in male genital organs, increase in weight of mammary glands and reproductive organs in women and premature breast developing in girls[7]

 

  • 陰虛 津液不足者慎用。

  • 體質太虛弱的孕婦忌服或慎服, 因為本品有雌激素樣的活性。[7]

  • 不宜和氟 喹諾酮類藥物同用。

  • 本品的雌激素活性可能引起一些副作用，如降低蛋白質濃度和男性生殖器官的酸性和鹼性磷酸酶，乳腺增大及生殖器官婦女和女孩乳房過早發育[7]
