Torticollis 

 

 by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu    按 此 看 中 文

 

Simple torticollis should be differentiated from atlantoaxial subluxation.

Atlantoaxial Subluxation

High incidences among children of 2 - 12 years old.

 

Causes includes:

 

  • arthritic
    • rheumatoid arthritis
    • juvenile rheumatoid arthritis
    • psoriatic arthritis
    • ankylosing spondylitis (chronic inflammationof the spine and the sacroiliac joints)
    • systemic lupus (SLE)

       

  • congenital
    • Down syndrome (20%)
    • Morquio disease
    • atlanto-occipital fusion
    • congenital absence/hypoplasia of dens (2/3 have h/o trauma)

       

  • infectious
    • retropharyngeal abscess

       

  • traumatic

    摘 要:
    落枕又稱“失枕”，是由於頸項部軟組織勞損、扭錯、受寒等原因，導致頸項部某些肌肉痙攣而產生疼痛、功能活動受限、斜頸、頸部強硬等一系列症狀。筆者在臨 床上常常點按少澤穴治療落枕，收效顯著。

    少澤穴(小腸經1) Shao ze(SI1): 位於小指末節尺側，離指甲角0.1寸。
