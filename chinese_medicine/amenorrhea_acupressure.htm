Amenorrhea, acupressure  閉經按摩法  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

 

Method:

  1. Let patient lie face up and practitioner should sit on the right side of the patient. Massage lower abdomen counterclockwise with slow and deep movements. At the same time, press and  rub quan yuan point and qi hai point about 10 minutes. Then massage qi hai point,  san yin jiao point, zu san li point for about 2 minutes per point.

  2. Let patient lie face down. The practitioner can use one finger chan method to massage the both sides lumbar spine, focusing on  gan shu, pi shu, shen shu, 1 to 2 minutes per point, or use rolling method on both sides of the lumbar spine, and then knead 2 to 3 times of the above points until the patient feels some soreness.

 

 

Locations of Points

 

xue hai (sea of blood): when the patient's knee is bent, cup your right palm to his left knee, with the thumb on the medial side and with the other four fingers directed proximally, and the thumb forming an angle of 45° with the index finger. The point is where the tip of your thumb rests.

 

qi hai point (sea of qi) On the front midline, 1.5 cun below the naval.

guan yuan point: On the front midline, 3 cun below the navel.

san yin jiao point: 3 cun superior to the medial malleolus, on the posterior border of the tibia.

Note: cun is the length of the middle joint of the middle finger. Literally means inch. 

讓患者仰臥，術者坐於右側，用摩法逆時針治療小腹，手法要求深沉緩慢，同時按揉關元、氣海約10分鐘。按揉血海、三陰交、足三裡，每穴約2分鐘。

讓患者俯臥，術者用一指禪推法，治療腰部脊柱兩旁，重點在肝俞、脾俞、腎俞，每穴1~2分鐘，或用滾法在腰脊柱兩旁治療，然後再按、揉上述穴位2~3遍，以病人感覺酸脹為度。
