Xin Qi Xu ( heart qi deficient)

心氣虛

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Please set your browser to read traditional Chinese BIG5.

Xin qi xu is traditional Chinese term used in diagnostics.

The clinical symptoms include:

Pale complexion, pale tongue with white fur, weak pulse,

palpitation, heaviness in chest area and short of breath,

especially after physical activities, and instant sweating.

See qigong therapy

 

心氣虛的症狀﹕

面色淡白，舌淡苔白，脈虛，心悸怔忡，胸悶氣短，活動後加重，自汗。
