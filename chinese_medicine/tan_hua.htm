tán huā 曇花                  (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    朱 興國編 譯   

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 Flos Epiphylli

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學 名﹕

 

Epiphyllum oxypetalum (DC.) Haw.

 

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

  gekkabejin (月下美人)[1]

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

  

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

  taam4 fa1

Other Names:

別名﹕

 qiong hua 瓊花，feng hua 鳳花，jin gou lian 金鉤 蓮，yue xia mei ren (beauty under the moonlight) 月下 美人。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

  Dutchman's pipe [1], Dutchman's pipe cactus [2]

Distribution:

產地﹕

 All over China

中國各地

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

 neutral, bland, slightly cold.

平，淡，微寒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

 heart, lung.

心，肺。
Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 For treatment of cough due to heat in the lung, consumption, spitting out blood, bleeding not during menses, palpitation, imsomnia.

清肺止咳，凉血止血，養心安神。主肺熱咳 嗽；肺痨；咯血；崩漏；心悸；失眠。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

  

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

 9~18 g

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

  

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

  

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

 do nott drink the raw juice of the herb in case of pixu (spleen deficient).

脾虛者 不可服鮮汁。
