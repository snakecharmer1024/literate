chuan jiao 川椒,jiao mu  椒目 (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Present Day Applications:

現代 應用﹕

jiao mu (seeds)﹕

  1. for lowering blood pressure

  2. for treating asthma, the effect is fast and effective (94.2%), toxicity is minor.

椒目﹕

  1. 降 血壓﹐

  2. 椒 目治療哮喘起效快﹐療效顯著 ﹐ 毒副作用小。對气 喘患者平均 有效率達94.2%。

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Semen Zanthoxyli

Biological name:

拉丁文學名﹕

Zanthoxylum bungeanum Maxim. (花 椒的 乾燥成熟種子) 或

Zanthoxylum schinifolium Sieb.et Zucc.(青 椒的 乾燥成熟種子)

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

shokusho

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

ch'onch'o

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

cyun1 ziu1

Other Names:

別名﹕

hua jiao (fruit), chuan jiao (fruit), chuan jiao mu (seed)

花椒 (果)﹐ 川椒 (果)﹐ 川椒目 (種子)

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Sichuan pepper

Distribution:

產地﹕

Gansu, Hebei, Henan, Jiangsu, Liaoning, Ningxia, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Shandong, Shanxi, Sichuan, Xizang (Tibet), Yunnan, Zhejiang provinces of China.

甘肅，河北，河南，江蘇，遼寧，寧夏，青海，陝西，山東，山西，四川，西藏，雲南，浙江等省。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

acrid, spicy hot, fragrant, hot and toxic.

味辛辣﹐氣香。性熱﹐有毒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

jiao mu: spleen and bladder channels.

chuan jiao: spleen, stomach, and lung channels

椒目﹕脾 ﹐膀胱經。

川椒﹕入 脾，胃，肺經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

for treating edema, bloating of abdomen, abundance of phlegm, night sweat, tinnitus due to shenxu.

椒目﹕行 水，消腫。主治水腫腹 脹，痰飲喘咳，盗汗﹐腎 虚耳鳴等。

川 椒﹕(1)驅寒止痛：用于陰寒内盛之脘腹絞痛。(2)殺虫：用 于驅 蛔虫與 蛔虫所致之腹痛。常配烏梅。(3) 風濕(痺)
