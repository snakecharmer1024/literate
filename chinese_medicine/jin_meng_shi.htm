jin meng shi 金礞石

 

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

A manuscript... writing and translation in progress

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Micae Aureus Lapis

Latin name:

拉丁名﹕

Micae Aureus Lapis

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 gam1 mung4 sek6

Other Names:

別名 ﹕

Prescription names: meng shi 礞石，jin meng shi 金礞石，duan meng shi 煅礞石，duan jin meng shi 煅金礞石

處方名：礞石，金礞石，煅礞石，煅金礞石等。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Vermiculite Schist seu Hydrobiotite Schist

Distribution:

產地﹕

mainly being produced in Henan and Hebei.

河南、河北

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sweet, salty, and neutal.

甘，鹹，平。
　　

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

lung, heart and liver channels.

歸肺，心，肝經。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

it possesses phlegm expectorant and laxation effect

本品有祛痰、瀉下作用。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Rids of phlegm, calming emotion. Being used in stubborn phlegm that coagulates, epilepsy, mania, and spasm.

墜痰下氣，平肝鎮驚。用於頑痰膠結，咳逆喘急，癲癇發狂，煩躁胸悶，驚風抽搐。

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

contains siliconates of iron, magnesium and aluminium.

含鐵、鎂，鋁的硅酸鹽。

Dosage:

用量﹕

3 to 6 g. Mostly in pills or in powder form.

3～6g，多入丸散服。

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

 

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

not to use in cases of pregnancies, spleen deficient or qi defecient or blood deficient.

脾胃虛弱及孕婦忌服。《本草從新 》： “氣弱血虛者大忌。”
