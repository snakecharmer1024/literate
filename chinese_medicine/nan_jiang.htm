nan jiang 南薑   (in English and Chinese 中英 文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Rhizome Languas galangae

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Languas galanga (L.) Stuntz; Alpinia galanga (L.) Willd.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese

粵音﹕

naam4 goeng1

Other Names:

別名﹕

chao zhou jiang 潮州薑，hong dou kou 紅豆蔻，da gao liang jiang 大高良薑。Langkuas (Indonesian, 印尼語)。

Common Name:

英文名﹕

greater galangal

Distribution:

產地﹕

grassland areas of Southern China and Southeast Asia, and India.
Forests, scrub, grasslands; 100–1300 m. Fujian, Guangdong,
Guangxi, Hainan, Taiwan, Yunnan [India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Myan-
mar, Thailand, Vietnam]

 

中國南方草原地带，東南亞，與印度。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

acrid, warm.

辛，温。

Channels (meridians) entered: 【1】

 

歸經﹕【1】

stomach, lung.

胃，肺。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

inhibitory effect on  fungi.

inhibitory effect against Staphylococcus aureus

.the ingredient curcumin possesses anticancer effect.

1'-acetoxychavicol acetate can inhibit human immunodeficiency virus type I (HIV-I).[3]

1'-acetoxychavicol acetate can also inhibit tumor-promoter-induced Epstein-Barr virus activation.[4]  Click here fo more on the essential oil

A study on mice feeding ethanol extract of jiang huang and another group with nan jiang, the gain in weights of sexual organs and increased sperm motility and sperm counts were observed in both groups of extract-treated male mice, however, these changes were highly significant in the nan jiang ( Alpina. galanga) treated group. Both extracts failed to show any spermatotoxic effects.

抑制霉菌。

抑制金 黃色酿膿葡萄球菌

其有效成份薑黄素有抗癌作用。

分離得到 的化學成份 1′-乙酰氧基胡椒酚乙酸酯可抑制病毒 HIV-I。

1′-乙 酰氧基胡椒酚乙酸酯可抑制病毒 Epstein-Barr. 按此看南薑 揮發油更多资料。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Being used in catarrh (inflammatin of the muccus), flatulence of the digestive tract, bad digestion, bad circulation with cold and/or numb limbs, allergy (nose). flu with cough, chronic cough, pain in the stomach.

用於粘 膜炎，腸胃脹氣，消化不良，鼻 子過敏，血液循環不良而手腳冰冷，手腳酸麻，感冒咳嗽，久咳不逾，腸胃不適，胃痛。

根據另一研究，根含1,8-桉葉油醇 (39%)，-蒎 烯 (11.9%). 葉含1,8-桉葉油醇 (32.5%)，-蒎 烯 (22.7%)，樟腦 (12,8%) 為主要成份。. 【2】
