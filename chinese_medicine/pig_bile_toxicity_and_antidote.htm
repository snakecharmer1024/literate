Pig‘s Bile Toxicity and Antidote  服用猪膽中毒解救法

Translated by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Pigs' bile is toxic.  Presently there is no effective antidote. In serious cases it can cause exhaustion of multi organs. If after taking pig's bile and nausea and/or vomiting occur, seek medical help. Induce vomiting, perform lavaging, induce diarrhea. Rid of the toxic residue as much as possible. In early stages use glucocorticoid, and diammonium glycyrrhizinate which may lower the jaundice and protect the liver. If possible use hemodialysis or plasma exchange to improve success rate.

服用猪膽中毒解救法

豬膽有毒。目前尚無特效解毒劑。嚴重者將導致肝腎等多器官功能衰竭。若服豬膽後出現惡心、嘔吐等症狀應盡快就醫，徹底催吐、洗胃、導瀉，盡可能清除和排出殘留的毒物。早期應用糖皮質激素、肝利欣等，能降低黃疸，保護肝功能。有條件者應盡早爭取實施血液透析、血液灌流或血漿置換，有利於提高搶救的成功率。
