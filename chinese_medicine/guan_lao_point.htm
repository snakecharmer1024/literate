guan lao point 顴顟 (窌)    SI18  (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 

Translated by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 朱興國編譯

 

guan lao: The word "guan" is referred to "cheek bone". Lao is an indentation. It is the indentation on the highest point of the cheek bone.

Location: from corner of the eye and go straight down to the cheek bone to locate the point.

Application: spastic facial nerves, drooping eyes and mouth, tooth ache and trigeminal neuralgia..

 

顴窌： “顴”指顴骨；“窌”指骨的凹陷處，顴骨最高點的下凹陷處，故名顴窌。
位置：由目外眦直下取之。
主治：面神經痙攣、口眼歪斜、牙痛、三叉神經痛，臉部神經麻痺。
