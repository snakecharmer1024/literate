beta-blockers :

Propanolol  is commonly used.

Brand names:

Betapace;Blocadren; Cartrol; Corgard; Inderal; Inderal LA; Kerlone; Levatol; Lopressor; Normodyne; Sectral; Tenormin; Toprol-XL; Trandate; Visken; Zebeta.

Note: This group of drugs may not suitable for people diagnosed with shenyangxu in Traditional Chinese medicine.

Other drugs:

Calcium channel blocker:  

Akilen; Anpec; Apoacor; Apo-Verap; Azupamil; Berkatens; Calan, Calan SR; Calaptin; Calaptin 240 SR; Cardiabeltin; Caveril; Civicor; Civicor Retard; Coraver; Cordilox; Cordilox SR; Corpamil; Covera-Hs; Dilacoran; Dilacoran HTA; Flamon; Geangin; Hexasoptin; Hexasoptin Retard; Ikacor; Ikapress; Iso-Card SR; Isoptin; Isoptin Retard; Isoptin SR; Isoptine; Isoptino; Manidon; Manidon Retard; Novo-Veramil; Quasar; Ravamil SR; Securon; Univer; Vasolan; Vasomil; Vasopten; Veracaps SR; Veracor; Verahexal; Veraloc; Veramex; Veramil; Verapin; Verapress 240 SR; Verdilac; Verelan; Verelan SR; Verpal; Verpamil; Vetrimil.

Note: This group of drugs also may not suitable for people diagnosed with shenyangxu in Traditional Chinese medicine.

Anti depressants:

Monoamine Oxidase (MAO) Inhibitors

 isocarboxazid, phenelzine, tranylcypromine

Tricyclics

 amitriptyline, imipramine, doxepin

Heterocyclics

 trazodone, maprotiline, bupropion

Serotonin-reuptake Inhibitors

 fluoxetine HCl (Adofen; Affectine; Alzac 20; Ansilan; Deproxin; Erocap; Fluctin; Fluctine; Fludac; Flufran; Flunil; Fluoxac; Fluoxeren; Fluoxil; Fluox-Puren; Flutin; Flutine; Fluxen; Fluxil; Fontex; Foxetin; Lorien; Lovan; Margrilan; Modipran; Neupax; Nopres; Nuzak; Oxedep; Pragmaten; Prizma; Proctin; Prodep; Prozac; Prozac 20; Rowexetina; Sanzur; Sinzac; Zactin; Zepax),

paroxetine HCl (Aropax 20; Deroxat; Paroxet; Paxil; Seroxat; Tagonis),

sertraline HCl (Altruline; Atruline; Dominum; Gladem; Lesefer; Lustral; Sosser; Zolof; Zoloft)

These drugs selectively effect neurotransmitter mechanisms in the central nervous system.

divalproex
