Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

P. amaryllifolius contains lectin with anti-human viruses like: herpes simplex virus type 1 (HSV-1), influenza virus (H1N1) [2]

P. amaryllifolius ethanol extract in vitro induced apoptosis on hormone independent breast cancer cell line MDA-MB-231. [1]

The leaf extract can significantly retarded oil oxidation and deterioration.

香林投所含的凝集素具有抗 人類病毒，如：單純性疱疹病毒１型(HSV-1)，感冒 病毒(H1N1)的作用。[2]

香林投的乙醇提取物，能使 非依賴激素性乳腺癌細胞系MDA-MB-231在體外誘導的細胞凋亡。 [1]

香林投葉子的提取物能延遲食物油的氧化和腐壞。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

 

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

Leaves contain non-specific lipid-transfer protein, lectins, alkaloids, 6E-pandanamine, pandamarilactonine-C, pandamarilactonine-D, pandamarilactonine-A, pandamarilactonine-B, diaportheone A, diaportheone B.
The leaves of some Pandanus species are known to contain calcium oxalate crystals. The root contains pandamarilactonine-H.

Dosage:

用 量﹕

For oral consumption: decoction, 15 ~ 30g, Fresh herb 60 ~ 120g; or squeeze for juice and mix with honey and take orally or make into juice and gargle.
External use: appropriate amount, wash, smash and apply topically or washed affected areas or sun dried and make into powder and make into dressing or topical ointment or cream ​​for applying to affected areas.

內服：煎湯，15~30g，鮮者60~120g；或絞汁蜜調服或搗汁含漱。外用： 適量，洗淨，攪爛外敷或煎水洗或晒乾研末調敷或製成外用藥膏或面霜作塗敷用。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉 例﹕

Hepatitis with jaundice: xi shu guo (noni fruit) 30 g, sabah snake grass (you dun cao) 30 克，

fresh pandan leaves 60 克. Boil with water and take orally. (Source: Hulu.com, Section of Experienced Formulae)* See Possible Toxicity and Cautions:.

To blacken hair : pandan leaves,  as much as 7 sheets, washed and cut into pieces .
Boil with 1 liter of water until the color is green . Let the decoction sit
overnight before cooking water . The next morning ,  squeeze 3 ripe noni fruitt to get juice. Mix with the leaf decoction made earlier. Use the mixture to wash hair . Do it 3 times
week , until you see the results. (translated from Indonesian (Translated from[4])

黃疸型肝炎：諾利果30gm，沙巴蛇草(憂遁 草)30gm，香林投鮮 葉60gm，水煎服。(葫蘆網．驗方組) * 看可能對肝臟有毒。

黑髮方：香蘭葉 (香林投葉)，洗淨，切成片，可多達7張。
加水1公升，燒開，燉直到水色成綠色。將湯汁放過夜。第二天早上，將成熟的諾麗果(檄樹 果) 擠汁。混合混入香蘭葉煎劑。使用混合物洗頭。每週三次，直到頭髮變黑。 （編者譯自印尼農業部網站[4]）

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

The leaves of some Pandanus species are known to contain calcium oxalate crystals. It may be harmful to those with decreased kidney function.

有些香林投種類的葉子含草酸鈣晶體，對腎臟功能不佳者可能不利。

Possible liver toxicity of noni fruit:
The Panel on Dietetic Products, Nutrition and Allergies of 
European Food Safety Authority concludes that ‘Morinda 
citrifolia (Noni) fruit puree and concentrate as novel food 
ingredients’ under the specified conditions are considered 
safe for the general population. 
However the Panel considers that the increasing number of
 case reports might indicate that some individuals have 
a particular sensitivity for hepatotoxic effects to noni fruit 
products. 
 
Noni fruit (and its products) are listed by the U.S. State Food 
and Drug Administration (FDA) as toxic items.
There are reports that:
(1) those with renal dysfunction had higher chance for renal 
damage.
(2) more cases of acute hepatitis have been reported after 
consumption of noni products.
 
諾利果可能對肝臟有毒：
歐洲食品安全局的飲食產品，營養和過敏專家小組
得出結論認為諾麗諾麗果漿和濃縮新型食品
對於一般人群，在特定條件下被認為是安全的。
然而，小組認為，越來越多的病例報告可能表明，
一些人對諾麗果產品的肝毒性作用特別敏感。
 
諾利果(及其產品)被美國國家藥物食品管理局(FDA
)列為有毒植物。
有報告指出：
(1)腎功能低下的人，更會造成腎功能的傷害。
(2)多例在食用諾麗產品後，造成急性肝炎。
