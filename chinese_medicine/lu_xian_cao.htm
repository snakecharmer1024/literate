Dosage:

用量﹕

9～15g。

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

Urinary tract infection, folk formula: fresh lu xian cao 60 g. Simmer with 3 bowls of water for 20 minutes. Save the liquid and add 2 and a half bowls of water and simmer for another 20 minutes. Mix the 2 batches of liquid and make into 2 servings for morning and afternoon.

鹿銜草60克。  水煎2次，混合後分上，下午服。 每日1剤。
