Hepatitis B, with elevated AST and ALT   (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu                按此看中文

liver qi stagnation with spleen dampness

Syndrome:

depressed, do not like to talk, fullness in chest and in rib cage,

sighing often, lack of libido, red tongue with white fur, bulging and dropping

sensation in the lower abdomen and pain, lack of appetite, limbs lack of

strength, frequent diarrhea, and stringy pulse.

Prescription:

wu wi zi 10 g, bai shao 12-15 g,

lian qiao 15 g, yu jin 10 g,

bai hua she she cao 30 g,

raw san qi powder 3 g (add to tea when ready-do not cook)

Adjustments:

For qixu (qi deficient): add dang shen, bai zhu, huang jing

For yinxu (yin deficient) add: gui ban, sheng di huang

For yangxu (yang deficient)) add: ba ji tian

For heavy dampness: add fu ling

　

乙型肝炎，肝氣鬱結，脾濕，轉氨酶升高
