Yin Enhancing Herbs 養 陰 藥

by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu

百 合 bai he

白 芍 bai shao

鱉 甲 bie jia

甘蔗梢 gan zhe shao

枸 杞 子 gou qi zi

龜 板 ， 龜 板 膠 gui ban， gui ban jiao

黃 精 huang jing

蓮子 lian zi (lotus seed)

蘆根 lu gen

麥 冬 mai dong

女 貞 子 nu zhen zi

胡 麻 仁 （芝麻）hu ma ren

沙 參 sha shen

石 斛 shi hu

天門冬 tian men dong

玉竹 yu zhu

Note: Chinese herbs are not used individually, or one at a time. They are used in combinations based on diagnostics. Also see liu wei di huang wan.
