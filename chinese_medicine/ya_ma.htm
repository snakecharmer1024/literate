ya ma (flax seed) 亞麻仁 (in English and Chinese 中英文)

flax root  亞麻根

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Present Day Applications:

現代應用﹕

anti-cancer: slows the cancer cell growth in breast cancer ;

preventing cardiovascular diseases and cerebral vascular diseases.

for chronic inflammation therapy.

防癌﹕抑制乳癌細胞繁殖。按 此看英文版報告。

防心脑血管病

治療慢性炎症

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

Ya ma ren (flax seed) and its root contain large amount of lignan which is a strong anti-oxidant and can inhibit the production of  harmful carcinogenic substances.

Anti-cancer of the intestine effect:: ya ma ren (flax seed) contains large amount of soluble fiber which can reduce the toxicity of the digested substances and speed up the secretion of the feces, thus reducing the duration of the time of stimulating substance on the mucous membrane of the intestine.

Ya ma root can help the function of the kidneys to excrete toxins.

Ya ma root contains large amount of omega -3 fatty acid which can lower blood lipids and lower the LDL in the blood and can reduce arteriosclerosis.

Ya ma inhibits the growth of the prostate cells. In vitro studies showed that the lignan of the ya ma ren (flax seed) can inhibit the growth of the cells of the prostate gland itself.

亞麻和 其根部含大量的木酚素 (lignan) 。具有很强的抗氧化特性，能有效遏 制促使癌生成的有害化學物 質。

對防治 腸癌的作用﹕富含纤维素，可减輕腸道中消化物產生的不良影响﹐加快腸道中廢物的排泄，减 短對腸内黏膜有剌激性的物質在腸道中停留的时间。

亞麻根 有助于腎臟的排毒功能 。

亞麻根 富含的脂肪酸，可降低血脂，低血液中的低密度胆固醇 (LDL)﹐治疗血管硬化。

控制攝護腺細胞生長。 體外的實驗證明 亞麻仁木酚素(lignan)可 以控制攝護腺細胞本身之生長。

Actions & Uses:

主治﹕

  1. Improves bowel movement

  2. Lowers blood lipids.

  3. Seed must be ground for consumption because of the tough seed coat that is indigestible. The ground-up seeds should be taken with water since the mucilage absorbs 5 times the weight of the seed.

  4. It is not advisable to use flax seed in baked food since the fatty acid omega-3 cannot withstand high heat and can become carcinogenic.

Flax Seeds are the highest dietary source of  lignan. They contain 75 times the quantity of  Lignan compared to the other plants that contain next highest source of Plant Lignan. The majority of the Plant lignan component of flax seeds is located in the outer husk of the Flax Seed .

 

Dosage Per Day:

每日用量﹕

15 ~ 30 克。

(每克亞麻仁含 a-亞麻脂酸與4克黏液。)

1 - 3 heaping tablespoons (15 to 30g) per day.

(One tablespoon (20 ml or ~ 10 g) of flax seeds contains 4 grams of a-linolenic acid and 4 g of mucilage).

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

For high level of  low-density lipoprotein (LDL): ya ma root 50 g. Make into decoction. Take it every day for 4 weeks. The LDL may lower 8%.

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

Omega-3 is a highly unstable fat and can become rancid quickly and become carcinogenic. Do not use flax seed oil for deep fat frying because omega-3 cannot stand high heat. Store flax seed and flax oil in black container and refrigerate. Discard seed or oil that smells fishy or rancid, because that smell signals toxicity.

Seed should be washed before using to avoid the mold , Aspergillus niger,  which is toxic for the respiration system and the mold , Aspergillus flavus,  which is toxic for the liver.  These molds are commonly found in grains and are extremely toxic.

　

Linamarin, after hydrolysis, can release free hydrogen cyanide acid, which can suffocate and poison the cells.

亞麻苦甙（linamarin） ，經水解後可析出游 離態的氢氰酸，致組織细胞窒息中毒。

 

A diet rich in flax seed seems to reduce the size, aggressiveness and severity of tumors in mice that have been genetically engineered to develop prostate cancer, according to new research from Duke University Medical Center. And in 3 percent of the mice the flax seed diet kept them from getting the disease at all. (From news release issued by Duke University Medical Center).
