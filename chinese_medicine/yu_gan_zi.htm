yu gan zi 餘甘子  ( in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    朱興國編 譯

英文藥名﹕

fructus Phyllanthi

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

Phyllanthus emblica Linn.

日語發音﹕

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Ridding of infection and soothing the pharynx, promoting saliva and quenching thirst. Being use in treatment of influenza with fever, cough, pain in throat, diphtheria, thirst and irritably hot, hypertension.

清熱利咽；潤肺化痰；生津止渴。主感冒發熱；咳嗽；咽痛；白喉；煩熱口渴；高血壓

Daly Dosage:

每日用量﹕

Taking orally: in decoction 15-30g; or use fresh herb to make juice.

內服：煎湯，15-30g；或鮮品取汁。

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

For treatment of influenza with fever, cough, pain in throat, dry mouth and irritable thirst, deficiency of vitamin C: use fresh fructus Phyllanthi 10 to 30 pieces. Make into decoction and take orally.  (Source: Commonly Used Chinese Herb Booklet by Guangzhou Army)

For treatment of diphtheria:  fructus Phyllanthi 500 g, xuan shen 30 g, gan cao 30 g. Soak all herb in cool boiled drinking water till frost develops. Spread the frost on cotton paper and dry under the sun. Add long dan cao powder 6 g, bing pian 1.5 g, bai guo powder 15 g. Blow the powder into the throat.

For treatment of asthma: fructus Phyllanthi 21 pieces. First boil pig lung and heart. Discard the bubble floating on the decoction. Add fructus Phyllanthi (yu gan zi). Make into soup. Take orally.

治感冒發熱，咳嗽，咽喉痛，口乾煩渴，維生素C缺乏症：鮮餘甘子果十至三十個。水煎服，(廣州部隊《常用中草藥手冊》)

治白喉：滇橄欖一斤，玄參，甘草各一兩。冷開水泡至起霜花，取霜用棉紙鋪開晒乾後，加馬尾龍膽草粉二錢，冰片五分，炒白果仁粉五錢，吹喉用。

治哮喘：滇橄欖二十一個，先煮豬心肺，去浮沫再加橄欖煮熟連湯吃

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

The ascorbic acid (vitamin C) of this fruit is very stable because of the protective effect of the tannin acid of the fruit. [1]

餘甘子所含的丹寧酸對它所含的抗壞血酸 (維生素C)有保護作用，使其十分穩定而不易變壞。【1】

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Use with care in cases of spleen deficient.

脾虛者慎服。
 

[1] Biochem J. 1936 June; 30(6): 1014–1020.

[2] Tang Materia Medica《唐本草》, Ben Cao Tu Jing《本草圖經》

化學成份參考：《唐本草》,《本草圖經》
