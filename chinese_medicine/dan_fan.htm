dan fan (Hydrated Copper Sulfate)  膽礬   (in English and Chinese 中英文)

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

 

Chemical name:

學名﹕

Hydrated Copper Sulfate

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

 

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

 

Other Names:

別名﹕

ya chui lu dan fan 鴨嘴綠膽礬

Common Name:

英文名﹕

Chalcanthite

Distribution:

產地﹕

 Mainly produced in western part of China.

主產於中國西部。

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

sour, acrid, cold and toxic

酸﹐辛﹐寒；有毒。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

liver, gall bladder

肝﹐胆。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

 Promotes bile secretion.

利膽作用。

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

Internal use: for promoting vomiting, getting rid of excessive wind/cold type of phlegm, toxic material.

External use: for drying out wounds, getting rid of rotten part of boils, dislodging wind/cold type of phlegm, sore throat, epilepsy, eating toxic substances, infected eyes, boils in the mouth, infected gum, swollen boils unable to rupture.

内服催吐，涌吐風痰，毒物；

外用解毒收濕，蝕瘡去腐。用于風痰堵塞，喉痹，癲癇，誤食毒物，風眼赤爛，口瘡，牙疳，腫毒不破等。

 

Chemical ingredients:

化學成份﹕

copper sulfate (CuSO4·5H2O)

硫 酸銅

Dosage:

用量﹕

0.3-0.6 g

Samples of formulae

:處方舉例﹕

To Promote Vomiting: dan fan 3 g, gua di 3 g. Make into fine powder. Take with water. (can be used in ingesting poison by accident)

催吐﹕膽礬 3克﹐瓜蒂 3克研成細末一次冲服。

Modern Research:

現代研究﹕

 

Cautions:

禁忌﹕

Not to use when the body is weak.

體虛者忌服。
