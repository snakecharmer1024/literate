tian xian (non classical formula)

Edited by Joe Hing Kwok Chu

Tian xian formula is a recent product that is being used in cancer therapy.

It is manufactured in capsules or in liquid form. If you are in the United States, please check with FDA to make sure it has been approved before prescribing it.  

The main ingredients consist of the following herbs:

bai zhu, gan cao, ren shen, tian hua fen, wei ling xian

 

天仙

主要成份為：

 

  白朮，甘草 ，人参 ，天花粉 ，  威靈仙

 

Click to go to the ingredients of Tian Xian Liquid.

 

See the report of from Taiwan on the medicine. It did not pass inspection of the Food and Drug of Taiwan government.
