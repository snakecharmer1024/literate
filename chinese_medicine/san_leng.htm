san leng  三 棱          (in English and Chinese 中英文)

 

Edited by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Rhizoma Sparganii Stoloniferi

Botanical name:

學名﹕

Sparganium stoloniferum Buch. -Ham. (Commercial name: jing san leng 荊 三 棱;  )

Scirpus yagara Ohwi (Commercial name: hei san leng, 黑 三 棱 )

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

sanryo

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

samnung

Pron. in Cantonese:

粵音﹕

sam leng

Other Names:

別名﹕

prescription names: jing san leng 荆三棱, san leng  三棱, hei san leng  黑三棱, cu san leng  醋三棱, zhi san leng  炙三棱﹐jing san leng 京三稜，ban tian dan dan 半天丹丹，xiao jian du wei 削尖都尉。。

(因 古时產于荆楚地域，此 藥 材 習 慣 稱 為 “荆三棱”。 )

Common Name:

英文名﹕

bur-reed rhizome, scirpus

Distribution:

產地﹕

Hebei, Liaoning, Jiangxi, Jiangsu.

河北, 遼 寧 , 江西, 江 蘇 等地，

Properties

(characteristics)﹕

性味﹕

acrid, bitter, neutral

辛,苦,平 。

Channels (meridians) entered:

 

歸經﹕

liver, spleen.

入肝, 脾經 。

 

Medical functions:

 

 

 

 

藥理﹕

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

its decoction

  1. can intensify the the contraction and tension in vitro intestine of rabbit

  2. can stimulate the in vitro uterus of rabbit

  3. can inhibit the platelet coagulation of rats,

  4. can extend the time of thrombosis, reducing the time of blood clots. The inhibition of thrombosis, lowering the  blood viscosity was stronger than the  inhibition of platelet aggregation

其煎劑

  1. 能引起對家兔 離體小腸收縮加強，緊張性升高。

  2. 對離體兔子宮也有興奮作用。

  3. 對大白鼠血液凝固的影響：有抑制血小板聚集，延長血栓形成時間，縮短血栓。其抑制血栓形成，降低全血 粘度的作用強於本品對血小板聚集功能的抑 制

Actions & Indications:

主治﹕

  1. painful menses. postpartum complication, amenorrhea cause by qi stagnation and/or blood stasis.  

  2. cu san leng (prepared with vinegar) is stronger in pain inhibition.

  3. fu san leng (stir fried with flour) is milder and is being used in promoting digestion.

 

  1. 月經痛，產後症候群， 氣滯血 淤所致之閉 經。

  2. 醋制三棱 ，引藥入肝，增强破血軟堅和止痛作用。

  3. 麸炒三 棱，性較緩 ， 助消食積 。

Dosage:

用量﹕

3 to 9 g in decoction.

3-9克，煎服。
   

Samples of formulae:

處方舉例﹕

For indigestion and flatulence: san leng 9 g, lai fu zi 9 g. Boil with water and use as tea. [1]

治食積腹脹：三棱，萊菔子各九克。水煎服。[1]

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

not to use in cases of pregnancy and menorrhagia (excessive bleeding during menses). Not to use in case of spleen deficient without blood stasis.

月經 過多者及孕婦忌用。脾 虛無淤忌 用。

 

Note: the 1985 edition of Chinese Medicine Dictionary, san leng is listed as hei san leng.
