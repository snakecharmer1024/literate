yang gan ju  洋甘菊

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu    朱興國編 譯     (in English and Chinese 中英文)

Pharmaceutical name:

英文藥名﹕

Flos Matricariae recutitae

Botanical name:

拉丁生物學名﹕

Matricaria recutita L.

Pron. in Japanese:

日語發音﹕

kamitsure (加密列)

Pron. in Korean:

韓語發音﹕

kamille (카밀레)

Pron. in Cantonese::

粵音﹕

joeng4 gam1 guk1

Other Names:

別名﹕

mu ju 母菊, German gan ju 德國甘菊, gan ju 甘菊

Common Name:

英文名﹕

German chamomile, wild chamomile, Hungarian chamomile, pineapple weed.

Distribution:

產地﹕

originally in Asia minor, Americas, Australia, now in northern and western parts of Xinjiang, Taiwan, Beijing and Shanghai.

分佈於小亞細亞、南、北美洲、澳洲大陸為原生地。現 在新疆北部和西部,，台湾，北京和上海

Properties

characteristics﹕

性味﹕

neutral, sweet, slightly bitter.

平，甘，微苦。

Channels meridians entered:

 

歸經﹕

heart, stomach

心，胃。

Medical functions:

藥理﹕

 

  1. antispasmodic,
  2. anxiolytic,
  3. anti-inflammatory,
  4. anti-ulcer,
  5. antibiotic: the essential oil, in vitro, show inhibiting effect on Staphylococcus aureus, Mycobacterium B6, Michigan, Corynebacterium, Candida albicans,
  6. antipruritic,  
  7. regulates immune system
  8. Inhibition of morphine dependence in rats and reduce the symptoms of withdrawal syndrome[31]
  9. antioxidant
  10. anti-parasites
  11. anti-brain injury
  12. antimutagenic
  13. cholesterol-lowering effects [16]

 

  1. 解痙

  2. 鎮靜、抗焦慮

  3. 抗炎

  4. 抗潰瘍

  5. 抗菌:母菊精油體外對金黃色葡萄球菌、分枝桿菌 B6、密執安棒桿菌、白色念珠菌等有抗菌作用[25-26]。

  6. 止癢[17]、抗過敏

  7. 免疫調節功能

  8. 體外實驗表明，母菊對人主要的藥物代謝酶有抑制作用[30]。

  9. 抑制大鼠對嗎啡的依賴性並減輕戒斷綜合徵的症狀[31]。

  10. 具有抗氧化[32]、

  11. 抗寄生蟲[33]。

  12. 抗腦損傷：

  13.  抗 突變

  14.  降 膽固醇

主治﹕

irritable bowel syndrome, stomach pain,

Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Use this herb with care  if you are allergic  to plants in the Asteraceae (Compositae) family like aster, chrysanthemum, mugwort, ragweed, and ragwort. or using blood thinner like warfarin.

可能引起膚過敏發炎，對菊科植物, (如 紫菀，菊花，艾 ，豚草，千里光)過敏的人，或用血液稀釋剤如華法林(warfarin)用 甘菊時需特別注意。
